Short Bio & Contact

Juliano is bachelor in Computer Science and Business Administration. He took his master's degree in Computer Science at Federal University of Santa Catarina and his D.Sc. degree in computer science at Fluminense Federal University, Brazil. Currently, he serves as professor in the computer networks undergraduate course at Federal University of Santa Maria, Brazil.
